## Detailed Observations

### January Performance
Generally solid month. One critical incident was handled well:
- Issue: Database cluster failover
- Response: 45 minutes to resolution
- Vendor follow-up: Excellent post-mortem documentation
- Changes implemented: Enhanced monitoring, configuration audit

**Takeaway**: Vendor demonstrates maturity in incident management.

### February Performance
Challenging month but important context:
- 8 total incidents (highest in quarter)
- 2 critical incidents
- **KEY**: 5 out of 8 incidents were related to external ISP routing issues
- Vendor proactively coordinated with ISP on our behalf
- Proposed secondary ISP connection (we declined due to cost)

**Takeaway**: Vendor handled external factors well but unable to control upstream issues.

### March Performance
Strong recovery:
- Only 3 incidents total
- Zero critical incidents
- Uptime back to 99.6%
- User satisfaction improved (4.8/5.0)

**Takeaway**: February appears to be anomaly, not trend.
